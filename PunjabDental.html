ABOUT US

Welcome to the Punjabi Dental Society, we are comprised of a group of passionate dentists who very strongly believe in Continuing Education. It is Continuing Education that keeps the practicing dentists, who have graduated years ago and those who have graduated recently, informed of the latest dental techniques, innovations and recent developments in dentistry.

This education enhances your personal dental careers and helps serve the community you are working within. Punjabi Dental Society is dedicated to bring quality C.E. seminars at affordable prices. The society conducts these seminars on Sundays, which make it convenient for the practicing dentists to attend without having to worry about closing their practices on a working day.
